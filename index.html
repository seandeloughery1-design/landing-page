FAQ

Common Questions

I've never heard of cold email — how does it actually work?
Cold email is simply the process of sending personalised, one-to-one emails directly to decision-makers at companies you want to work with. Unlike spam or mass marketing, each message is tailored to the recipient. When done correctly it's one of the most effective ways to start commercial conversations at scale — and we handle the entire process for you.
How quickly will I start seeing booked appointments?
Most clients see their first booked meetings within 2–3 weeks of launch.
What's the difference between a lead and a booked appointment?
A lead is just contact information. A booked appointment is a confirmed meeting with a decision-maker who has said they're interested. You only pay for the latter.
Do I need a big budget to get started?
No. There's a small setup fee, then you pay per meeting that shows up. No ad spend, no retainer.
Do you require long-term contracts?
No. We work month to month. If we're not delivering, you're not paying.
What if a meeting isn't qualified or doesn't show up?
You don't pay for it. Simple as that.
What do you mean by qualified?
A decision-maker at a company that matches your ideal client profile, who has confirmed interest and shown up to the call.
